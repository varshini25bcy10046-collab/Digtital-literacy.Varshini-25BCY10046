## Project Overview
This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio developed as part of the CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal University. The project focuses on building essential digital skills such as online presence, professional communication, collaboration tools, and cyber safety awareness.

---

## Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
In this task, I created a one-page infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy. The infographic covers important topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, and maintaining a professional online presence. This task helped me understand how to present information visually in a clear and engaging way.

---

## Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
For this task, I built my digital presence by creating and updating profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms are important for showcasing skills, connecting with professionals, and accessing learning opportunities. This task helped me understand the importance of maintaining a professional online identity.

---

## Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
In this task, I explored coding and collaboration tools. I completed a beginner-level coding challenge on a practice platform to improve my problem-solving skills. Additionally, I created a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms and linked it to a response sheet. This task helped me learn how to use online tools for collaboration and data collection.

## Task 4 – Email Etiquette
This task involved drafting two professional emails — one requesting an assignment extension and another expressing interest in an internship. I also created a checklist of do’s and don’ts for responsible social media usage. This task improved my understanding of formal communication and digital etiquette.

---

## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
In this task, I studied a cybercrime case (such as phishing or online fraud) and explained how it occurs, who it targets, and its consequences. I also created a prevention checklist with safety tips, including UPI and financial security measures. This task helped me become more aware of online threats and how to stay safe.

## Project Report
The detailed project report is included in the report folder, which contains explanations, reflections, and learnings from all five tasks.
